# Safe Trello Card Workflow

```text
User: Read Doing list, read-only. Do not change anything.
Agent: Summarizes cards and asks which one to work on.
User: Card #123.
Agent: Asks for approve card.
User: approve card.
Agent: Reads card details and proposes plan.
User: approve plan.
Agent: Edits only approved files.
Agent: Shows diff.
User: approve commit.
Agent: Commits.
User: approve push.
Agent: Pushes and comments on Trello.
```
